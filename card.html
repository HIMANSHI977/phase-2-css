AVOCADO TOAST

Fast and delicious toast for the whole family

Ingredients

  • 100 ml milk
  • 50 g butter
  • 150 g pumpkin
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 2 tbsp cinnamon
  • A pinch of salt
  • 30 leaves of thyme
  • 100 ml goat milk
  • 2 tbsp ground cinnamon

Directions

  • Toast the bread until crisp.
  • Cut the avocado and scoop out the flesh.
  • Mash the avocado with a fork.
  • Add a little salt, pepper, and lemon (optional).
  • Spread the mashed avocado on the toast.
  • Serve immediately.
